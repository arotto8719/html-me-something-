Despite my career change involving Launchcode, I will always have an interest with history. Downton Abbey is a great eaxmple of historical fiction. Our family has been obsessed with this British Drama when we started watching it during the airing of season 2. I also recently went with family and friends to the opening night of the movie. My love for British cooking also started because of Downton Abbey. Most of the dishes in the show, for upstairs and downstairs, you can make at home and are delicious. I would love to share what could be made easily with simple, everyday ingredients. Here are my favorite British foods to make that were inspired by Downton Abbey.


Kedegree: This breakfast dish with fish and rice that was influenced during the period of British Colonization of India. Cream of Mushroom Soup Creamy Russet Leek Soup Mrs. Patmore's Dropped Roasted Chicken Shepard's Pie Yorkshire Puddings Crepes Suzette British Battenburg Cake Treacle Tart Sybil's Ginger Nut Biscuits


Honorable Mention: Chestnuts Jam Tarts Soupe a l'Oignon (French Onion Soup) with Madalines Meat Pies
